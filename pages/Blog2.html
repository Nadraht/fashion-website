How to Dress for Your Body Shape

By Nadraht Mohammed | July 2025


Understanding your body shape is the first step to dressing with confidence. Whether you're curvy, athletic, petite, or full-figured, knowing how to highlight your strengths is key to looking polished and put together.



  1. Hourglass: Balance is your best friend. Accentuate your waist with fitted dresses, wrap tops, and high-rise trousers.

  2. Pear-Shaped: Draw attention upward with structured shoulders, bold necklines, and lighter tops. A-line skirts flatter your curves beautifully.

  3. Apple-Shaped: Create definition with V-neck tops, flowy blouses, and pieces that skim your middle. A good blazer works wonders.

  4. Rectangle: Add curves with peplum tops, belted dresses, and detailed sleeves. Layering also helps build dimension.

  5. Inverted Triangle: Soften your upper body with scoop necks and flowy tops. Go bold with wide-leg pants and flared skirts to balance proportions.

Remember: fashion isn't about hiding — it's about celebrating what makes you unique. Confidence is always the perfect fit.
