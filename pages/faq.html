Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions about our custom-fit services and fashion collections.

Q: How long does it take to get my order? ▼

A: All custom-fit orders are completed and delivered within 5 to 7 working days after confirmation of measurements and payment.

Q: Can I order based on my exact measurements? ▼

A: Absolutely! That's what makes Group 3 Fashion House special. You can either send your measurements through our contact form or request a virtual measurement guide.

Q: Do you offer refunds or exchanges? ▼

A: Yes, we offer exchanges for sizing issues within 3 days of delivery. However, because our products are made to order, refunds are only available for damaged or incorrect items.

Q: What sizes do you offer? ▼

A: We don't use standard sizes — we custom-fit every piece to your unique body shape. That way, you get a perfect fit every time.

Q: Where are you located or based? ▼

A: Group 3 Fashion House operates from Lagos, Nigeria. We serve clients across the country and deliver to all states.

Q: Do you deliver outside Nigeria? ▼

A: Yes, we do! Group 3 Fashion House offers both nationwide and international delivery through trusted courier partners. No matter where you are, your perfect fit can reach you.
